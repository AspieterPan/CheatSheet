## 链式求导

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\left(\begin{array}{ll}
\frac{\partial z}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial v}
\end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d} u}{\mathrm{~d} x} \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} v}{\mathrm{~d} x}
\end{array}\right)
$$
