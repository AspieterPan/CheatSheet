if $\mu = \phi(t),\nu = \psi(t),z = f[\phi(t),\psi(t)]$ , then
$$\frac{dz}{dt} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial \mu}\frac{d\mu}{dt} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial \nu}\frac{\partial \nu}{\partial t}$$
